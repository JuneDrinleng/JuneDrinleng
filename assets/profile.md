# About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Chemistry at Tsinghua University's Department of Chemistry, working under the advisorship of Prof. Yan He. My research combines deep learning with single-particle tracking to advance the understanding of biophysical processes in complex systems.

With a background in chemical physics, I've developed expertise in both experimental and computational approaches to studying particle dynamics. My current focus is on developing robust machine learning algorithms for trajectory analysis and understanding molecular interactions at the nanoscale.

Beyond research, I'm passionate about open science, code quality, and making computational tools accessible to the broader scientific community.
